My name is Mike Griffin. I'm married to my fantastic wife, Louise and I'm currently living in Celbridge, Co. Kildare. I work for the National Museum of Ireland, based out of Collins Barracks.

I play football with Shannon Gaels in Cavan. I also take part in triathlons, duathlons, aquathons and I'll try my hand at any other race if I think I can get away with it. I finished Ironman Lanzarote last year in under 11 hours.

I have represented Ireland in canoe polo at three European Championships and one World Championship. I played internationally from 1999 until 2005. I played with Groningen for a season in 2002 before coming back and and playing with Muppets in Ireland. I still play occasionally in Ireland. I am also an international referee.
